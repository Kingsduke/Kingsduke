⚜ How to Play Dukes Chess

  • Standard chess rules apply on a 9×9 board.
  • Back rank: Rook · Bishop · Knight · Queen · King · Duke · Bishop · Knight · Rook
  • Duke moves like a Queen but maximum 3 squares in any direction.
  • Duke captures only on square 2 or 3 — not the adjacent square.
  • Duke jumps — cannot be blocked by any piece on squares 1 or 2.
  • Pawns promote on the opposite back rank (Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight).
  • Win by checkmate. Draw by stalemate or threefold repetition.
